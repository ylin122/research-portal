name: News updates must be portfolio-specific
description: Industry & Market Updates section should only contain news directly tied to portfolio tickers, not general market/macro updates
type: feedback
---

Industry & Market Updates in the PA dashboard should ONLY contain news that directly impacts specific portfolio positions/tickers. No broad market news (e.g., "S&P +3%", "Iran ceasefire rally", generic macro commentary).

**Why:** User wants signal, not noise. Every entry should reference a specific ticker they hold or have exposure to (portfolio holdings, SMH/SOXX/DRAM constituents, or research tickers).

**How to apply:** When refreshing dashNewsUpdates, every entry must name at least one ticker the user holds. Remove or reframe any entry that's purely macro/market-level. If a macro event matters, frame it through the lens of specific position impact (e.g., "SMH +5%" not "market rallied").
