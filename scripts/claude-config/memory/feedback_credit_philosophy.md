EV matters most for margin of safety in credit — it is the ultimate fundamental discussion. Don't just focus on cash flow/debt service.

**Why:** User corrected a Pareto framing that said "80% of credit risk is FCF relative to debt service." The real 80/20 is understanding full enterprise value in a downside case and what recovery looks like if things go wrong. Highly valuable companies generally have access to liquidity as a bridge — EV is the backstop.

**How to apply:** When discussing credit risk, recovery analysis, or investment frameworks, always anchor on enterprise value and through-cycle thinking rather than just current-period cash flow coverage.
